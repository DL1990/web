House Specials

CMC's Dublin Coddle

The Irish Coddle, a cherished dish in Irish cuisine, epitomizes hearty and simple home cooking, particularly associated with Dublin. Layers of Irish sausages, bacon, potatoes, and onions meld together in a slow-cooked harmony, showcasing the essence of quality ingredients. Enhanced by herbs like thyme or parsley, this classic embodies Irish warmth and hospitality, making it a timeless symbol of traditional fare.

CMC's Irish Stew

Irish Stew, a quintessential dish in Irish culinary tradition, is a hearty and flavorful creation that has stood the test of time. Rooted in simplicity, this iconic stew typically features tender chunks of lamb or mutton, accompanied by potatoes, carrots, onions, and often, a medley of herbs. Slow-cooked to perfection, the stew allows the ingredients to meld, creating a rich and savory broth that embodies the essence of comfort food. The choice of quality ingredients, particularly succulent cuts of meat, contributes to the dish's robust flavor. Whether enjoyed on a chilly evening or as a centerpiece during festive gatherings.
